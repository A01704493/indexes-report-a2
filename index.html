Introduction

This report analyzes the performance of 10 major global stock market indices over the past decade, with a special focus on the best and worst performers.

Key Insights

  • Higher volatility doesn't always correlate with higher returns, contrary to classic financial theory in some cases.
  • Regional differences are significant, with developed markets often showing more stable risk-return profiles.
  • Indices with high technology exposure have generally shown better risk-adjusted returns.
